Privacy Policy

The Fart Gun

Last updated: 22 December 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how The Fart Gun (“the App”) handles information when you use it. The App is developed and published by Asif Imtiaz Ahmed, based in Canada.

We respect your privacy and are committed to transparency regarding data handling practices.

2. Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the App as distributed on:

  • Google Play Store (Android)
  • Apple App Store (iOS)

3. Information Collected by the App

3.1 Personal Data

The App does not collect, store, or process personal data.

The App does not collect:

  • Names or email addresses
  • User accounts or login credentials
  • Phone numbers
  • Location data
  • Contacts, photos, or media files

3.2 Local App Data

The App stores non-personal user preferences locally on your device only. This data never leaves the device and is deleted when the App is uninstalled.

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain information such as device data, advertising identifiers, and ad interaction metrics in accordance with Google’s Privacy Policy.

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4.2 Analytics and Crash Reporting

The App uses Firebase Crashlytics and limited analytics solely for stability and performance monitoring. These services may collect diagnostic information such as crash logs, device model, operating system version, and app version.

Firebase Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

4.3 Platform Services

The App is distributed via Google Play Services and the Apple App Store. These platforms may collect information independently according to their own privacy policies.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for a general audience. It does not knowingly collect personal data from children. There is no age verification mechanism in place. Ads are served through Google AdMob without developer-controlled age-based personalization settings.

6. Data Storage and Security

  • No personal data is stored on developer-controlled servers
  • The App functions entirely offline except for advertising
  • All locally stored data remains on the user’s device

7. User Rights and Legal Compliance

The App is designed to comply with major global privacy regulations, including GDPR (EU/UK), CCPA/CPRA (California), COPPA (United States), and PIPEDA (Canada).

Because the App does not collect personal data directly, most data access or deletion requests should be directed to the relevant third-party providers.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.
